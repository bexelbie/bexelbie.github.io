## Presented at [Flock 2023](https://flock2023.sched.com/) in Cork, Ireland on 2 August 2023

[Recording](https://www.youtube.com/live/bD2R4Yt8m88)

## Talk Description

While Red Hat is the primary sponsor of the Fedora and CentOS Projects, internally there is a bit of a split-brain syndrome where we separate our sponsorship from our wants and goals. This keynote focused on what Red Hat is thinking about when it looks at participation in the Fedora and CentOS Projects. Like all contributors, Red Hat has shown up to "scratch its own itch." So what are those itches? Let's explore some together.
